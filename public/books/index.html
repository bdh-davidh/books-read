2024

  • Nine Lives: My Time As MI6's Top Spy Inside al-Qaeda

    By Aimen Dean | Nonfiction

    A solid and down-to earth account account of the motivations, methods and mechanisms of al-Qaeda.

  • The Wager

    By David Grann | Nonfiction

    A well researched, engaging and interesting account of 18th century ships, shipwrecks, mutiny and murder.

  • Smiley's People

    By John le Carré | Fiction

    A fitting, understated, masterful conclusion to the Smiley Triology.

  • The Honourable Schoolboy

    By John le Carré | Fiction

    A complex, show don't tell thriller, that's not quite the author at his best.

  • The Escape Artist

    By Jonathan Freedland | Nonfiction

    A page turning, thriller of a book, dealing with a terrible subject.

  • The Sisters Brothers

    By Patrick deWitt | Fiction

    The Blues Brothers meet Deadwood. In a good way.

  • Victory City

    By Salman Rushdie | Fiction

    A thin, sketch of a story, with characters I didn't care about.

  • But What Can I Do?

    By Alastair Campbell | Nonfiction

    A great magazine article, on an important subject, stretched out to make a book.

  • Tinker Tailor Soldier Spy

    By John le Carré | Fiction

    A masterfully understated, slow-burn thrilling read.

  • Politics on the Edge

    By Rory Stewart | Nonfiction

    An interesting insight into the world of Westminster politics.

  • How to Make the Best Coffee at Home

    By James Hoffmann | Nonfiction

    A concise, no-nonsense guide to making better coffee. Useful and beautiful!

  • A Hero in France

    By Alan Furst | Fiction

    An atmospheric exploration of life in occupied France during the Second World war.

  • The Road

    By Cormac McCarthy | Fiction

    Amazing, horrifying and mesmerising.
